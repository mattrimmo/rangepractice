1

3
Pick a target and aim at it every shot. Not the same target every time — move it around. Left flag, middle flag, right flag. You're practising direction control, not just swinging into space.
4
Check Toptracer FTP every 10 balls. After balls 10, 20 and 30. You want the number staying consistent — not dropping from ball 1 to 10 then creeping back up. Consistency across 30 balls is the proof.
